Notes on possible usage:

blip - menu select move
bump - action/player blocked
enter1 - menu enter, go back, etc
pickup_* - pickup/jump sounds
step - character step (if we want...)
thud - place box, maybe also menu select sounds?
powerup1 - telekinesis
powerup2 - teleport
win - winning sound!
